About Image Painting CNY

Image Painting has been serving local residents and businesses since 2000 with quality painting services. We are dedicated to offering commercial and residential interior painting and exterior painting services that can improve the appearance and value of your home or business. We also do partial or full Apartment Gut Renovations. We can handle your entire Home Remodeling or Apartment Renovation project. All electrical, plumbing, cabinetry, stone work, tile, marble, granite, all carpentry, custom built ins, custom furniture, any custom woodworking, painting, doors, floors, etc.

Residential Painting

From new construction painting to remodeling projects for older homes, Image Painting is proud to help you achieve a new look for your house that complements your style and personality. Our professional painters can provide classic to contemporary, and specialty to faux finishes that define distinction. We understand that delivering excellent results sometimes involves more than just quality paint and professional painters; it may require additional preparations to arrive at the best look and feel for your environment. That's why, as an experienced painting contractor, we are happy to provide your home with the necessary reparations and prep work. We also offer the most comprehensive range of exterior and interior painting services to ensure a uniform look for the entire home.
